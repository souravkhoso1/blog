Every blog starts somewhere. This is mine.

I've been meaning to write publicly for a while. Not to build an audience, not to grow a personal brand — just to have a place where ideas can land and stay. Writing forces clarity that thinking alone doesn't.

## What this is

A personal blog. Mostly tech: code I find interesting, tools I'm using, problems I've solved (or failed to solve). Sometimes other things.

I'll try to keep posts short and concrete. If I can't explain it in a few paragraphs, I probably don't understand it well enough yet — and that's fine, I'll come back when I do.

## What this isn't

A content calendar. A tutorial factory. An SEO play.

Just me writing when I have something worth saying.
